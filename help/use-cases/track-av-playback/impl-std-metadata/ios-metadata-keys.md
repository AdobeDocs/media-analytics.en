| `ADBVideoMetadataKeyAD_LOAD`  | Ad load  | Video  |
| `ADBVideoMetadataKeyMVPD`  | mvpd  | Video  |

| `ADBVideoMetadataKeyDAY_PART`  | Day part  | Video  |
